Hobbies

Car Audio

Car Audio is an old hobby of mine from my high school days. I competed in both sound quality and SPL (Sound Pressure Level) competitions. My first install I paid to have done and it was spendy. I decided any installs after that that I would do by myself with the help of my brother Kenny. The best build I did was mounting four 15" subwoofers behind the front seats of a 4-door 1985 Ford Tempo. We gutted the interior and poured concrete in the floor pan. Cut holes through the roof and suspended concrete from it. Welding the doors shut and built a wooden dash. Made use of a dual alternator system (Ford Tempo with a hood scoop) and three batteries. It was so loud your eyes could not focus while driving with the stereo on.

Wood Working

Woodworking and building things with my hands brings me immense joy. I have made many projects over the years that include matching oak nightstands, a custom coffee table, a ferret enclosure from on old armoire. and of course, many car audio projects such as amp racks and subwoofer boxes.
